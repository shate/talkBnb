How does it work?

6 steps to hosting success:

step 1: Application

Apply online or call 855-8-TALKBNB (US toll free) for assistance.

step 2: Home visit

We schedule a visit to your place to check the accommodations or make additional photos.

step 3: Choice of guest

As soon as we have guests whose profiles fit yours, we will send you the guests’ profiles. You choose the one you like.

step 4: Guest’s arrival

The guest arrives the scheduled date. Hosts are not responsible for airport pick up.

step 5: Payment

The Guest pays in advance. The host receives the payment within a week after the guest arrival.

step 6: Ongoing support

We are there 24/7, 365 days! 855-8-TALKBNB (US toll free) support@talkbnb.com

Time is money, and we understand that down time is hard to come by. Speaking with your guest for 2-3 hours a day shouldn’t necessarily mean stealing this time from your family, business, friends and hobbies! Every day, we perform basic routine tasks, such as cooking a meal, doing laundry, or commuting to and from work. Imagine using this time wisely while spending it with your guest. These tedious tasks can eventually turn into an exciting highlight of your day by simply including your guest. You’ll get more out of your routine schedule without adding extra work. Become more efficient with your time, and get paid for it!

Talkbnb was designed to give people all over the world the freedom to learn a foreign language in a relaxed setting, without fear of judgment. People study a foreign language at school for years. They may know all the grammar rules and have a great vocabulary, yet they are too shy use their language skills freely when the opportunity presents itself. Learning a language can (and must be!) a captivating and stress-free process. When trying to speak a foreign language, mistakes are bound to happen. Whether it’s choosing the wrong word or struggling with correct pronunciation – it’s all part of the learning process. You will be able to correct your guests’ errors on the spot, providing encouragement that will increase their confidence and fluency.
